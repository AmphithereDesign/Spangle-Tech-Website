Our Story

Beginning in high school, Zach always had a strong interest in anything technology related or computer based. His pursuit of the technology industry led him to the discovery of Computer Networking in college and ignited his passion even further. While attending IUPUI, Zach developed a desire to learn more about the world of physical security which greatly assisted his already growing knowledge of cyber security.


After his studies, he was motivated to improve his community at large and indeed bring criminals and misconduct to justice by providing whatever physical security technology he could.


He now continues to provide his clients with state-of-the-art physical security cameras and secure networks all with the mental fortitude and expertise to help clients feel assured that, not just their physical property is safe, but their network is as well.
